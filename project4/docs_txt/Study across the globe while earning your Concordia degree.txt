The Concordia Student Exchange Program (CSEP) offers undergraduate and graduate students the opportunity to study for 1 or 2 terms at a partner institution across the globe while earning credits towards their Concordia degree. For the 2020-21 academic year: Choose among our destination countries available in Asia, Europe, Oceania, Africa, Middle East and the Americas Select among our 180+ available partner institutions Receive up to $6,000 to fund your term(s) abroad The deadline for applications is February 1, 2020, at 5 p.m. Apply now or learn more about CSEP.
