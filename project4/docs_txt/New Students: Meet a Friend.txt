Join us and take this opportunity to meet a friend via icebreakers and games facilitated by our Welcome Crew Mentors. You’ll also learn some tips and hacks on how to make friends at Concordia! This event has both virtual and in-person dates.  Note: Please be aware of the health and safety measures (such as mask requirements) outlined to protect yourself and others in the Concordia community when you come to campus for the in-person sessions. Capacity is limited, registration is required.
