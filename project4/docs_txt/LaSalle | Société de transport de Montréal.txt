Ouvrir la section: Locate the special shuttle stop sign Two stops are available at this station and the signs are easily identifiable since they are green, the color of the métro line they serve. Eastbound towards Honoré-Beaugrand: Bus stop 56687, located on Caisse, between LaSalle and Rushbrooke. Westbound towards Angrignon: Bus stop 56688, located on Caisse, between LaSalle and Rushbrooke. This shuttle is not free, the regular fare applies.  Close Find out more about the station Ouvrir la section: Parking space for bicycles Number of bicycle stands available outside the station: 42.  First come, first served. Before taking our public transit system with your bike, please read the Bicycles section for more info. Close Ouvrir la section: A little info about your métro station's history Architects: Gillon & Larouche. Opened: September 1978. Origin of name: boulevard LaSalle, paying tribute to explorer René-Robert Cavelier, Sieur de LaSalle (1643-1687), who spent his life searching for a route to China. Did you know? The slanted ceilings in this station are a reminder of those in the area's former factories. Artwork: mural by Peter Gnass.
