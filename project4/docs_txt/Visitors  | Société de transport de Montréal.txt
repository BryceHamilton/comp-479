If not, you can still use paratransit services if you use a wheelchair at all times. Visitors from outside Quebec Visitors from outside Quebec can use paratransit services if they use a wheelchair at all times. Create your STM paratransit client record Dial 1-514-280-8211 to create a client record with the paratransit customer service. You will have to provide the following information: First name Last name Date of birth Address Telephone number Email address (if applicable) File number from your municipality’s paratransit service provider if you are a visitor from elsewhere in Quebec registered for paratransit service. Use of mobility aids Transfer ability Date of visit Presence or absence of a companion Number of luggage items (if applicable) You will receive a client number for reserving trips. When boarding, you must provide this number to the driver. This is proof of your eligibility for paratransit services. During your visit, you must make trip reservations through an agent: By telephone: 514-280-8211, option 1 during operating hours Texte important Automated and online reservation services are not offered to visitors.
