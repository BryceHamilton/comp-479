Site search Concordia Directories Search John Molson School of Business Supply Chain and Business Technology Management The Department of Supply Chain and Business Technology Management at the John Molson School of Business, Concordia University strives to equip its students with an integrated set of decision-making skills. Meet our faculty and staffJob opportunities Rustam Vahidov Interim Chair of Supply Chain and Business Technology Management With the Faculty since 2000, Dr. Vahidov received his PhD in Decision Sciences from Georgia State University, Atlanta, GA, USA (2000), his MBA in Decision Sciences from Georgia State University, Atlanta, GA, U.S.A. (1997) and his BSc in Management Information Systems, Azerbaijan State Oil Academy, Baku, Azerbaijan (1991). He teaches undergraduate courses in Business Technology Management. His research focuses on Software Agent Negotiations, Decision Support Systems, Multi-Agent Systems, Electronic Negotiations, Fuzzy Logic, Genetic Algorithms, Neural Networks, and others.
