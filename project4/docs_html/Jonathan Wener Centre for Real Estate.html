Jonathan Wener Centre for RealEstate

JWCRE
Leadership
Our team
Research
Speaker series
Events

The Jonathan Wener Centre for Real Estate was established to:

  • Train the next generation of real estate professionals by providing students with the cutting-edge knowledge and real-world experience they need to succeed in the field
  • Drive innovation through knowledge creation and provide real estate leaders with insights into the evolving market and industry
  • Provide industry players and their employees with training and tools to adapt and excel

Current and future initiatives

  • Minor in Real Estate
  • Major or graduate program in real estate
  • Research and knowledge creation in the area of real estate
  • Real estate conference and speaker series
  • Real estate case competition
  • Co-ops and internships
  • Corporate training and certification programs
