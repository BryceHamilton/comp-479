Fall term 2021

    November 2021

  • Mon, Nov. 1
    Deadline to apply for admission to undergraduate programs, winter term 2022
  • Mon, Nov. 1
    Deadline to apply for degree transfer, winter term 2022
    (For currently registered students to transfer into the Faculty of Arts and Science or the Gina Cody School of Engineering and Computer Science)
  • Mon, Nov. 8
    Deadline for academic withdrawal (DISC) from fall‑term courses
    (Note: Deadline has been extended to December 7, 2021)
  • Fri, Nov. 12
    Deadline to register with the Access Centre for Students with Disabilities and receive exam accommodations for the fall 2021 final examination period
  • Thu, Nov. 25
    Registration start date for newly authorized independent students, winter term 2022
  • Mon, Nov. 29
    Last day for instructor‑scheduled tests or examinations

    • December 2021

  • Wed, Dec. 1
    Deadline to apply for Quebec resident status, fall term
  • Mon, Dec. 6
    Last day of classes, fall term

    Deadline for academic withdrawal (DISC) from fall‑term courses
  • Tue, Dec. 7
    Make‑up day for classes scheduled on Monday, October 11
  • Wed, Dec. 8
    Examinations begin
  • Wed, Dec. 22
    Aide financière aux études (AFE) end of funding, fall term
  • Wed, Dec. 22
    Examinations end
  • Thu, Dec. 23
    Holiday period, University closed (December 23 to January 5)

    Classes begin, winter term

    Deadline for submission of late‑completion work for courses ending in December 2021
    (application deadline February 1)
  • Mon, Feb. 28
    Replacement examinations begin
  • Mon, Feb. 28
    Supplemental examinations begin for courses ending in December 2021 (graduating students only)
  • Mon, Feb. 28
    Start of mid-term break

    • March 2022

  • Tue, Mar. 1
    Deadline to apply for admission to undergraduate programs, full‑time regular session 2022‑23
  • Tue, Mar. 1
    Deadline to apply for degree transfer, fall term 2022

  • Thu, Mar. 3

  • Fri, Mar. 4

  • Sun, Mar. 6
    End of mid-term break
  • Fri, Mar. 18
    Deadline to register with the Access Centre for Students with Disabilities and receive exam accommodations for the winter 2022 final examination period
  • Mon, Mar. 21
    Deadline for academic withdrawal (DISC) from two‑term and winter‑term courses
    (Note: Deadline has been extended to April 18, 2022)

    • April 2022

  • Fri, Apr. 1
    Deadline to apply for Quebec resident status, winter term 2022
  • Wed, Apr. 6
    Last day for instructor‑scheduled tests or examinations
  • Wed, Apr. 13
    Last day of classes, fall/winter and winter terms 2021-22
  • Fri, Apr. 15
    Easter holidays, University closed (April 15 to 18)
  • Mon, Apr. 18
    Deadline for academic withdrawal (DISC) from two‑term and winter‑term courses
  • Mon, Apr. 18
    Last day of Easter holidays, University closed
  • Tue, Apr. 19
    Examinations begin
  • Sat, Apr. 30
    Aide financière aux études (AFE) end of funding, winter term
