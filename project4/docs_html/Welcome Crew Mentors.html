Student Success Centre

THE WELCOME CREW

The Welcome Crew is made up of experienced student mentors who are here to help you successfully transition into your first year at Concordia.

CALL US at 514-848-2424, ext. 7369
EMAIL the Welcome Crew
JOIN the New Concordians 2021-22 Facebook group

Our team

We're a team of upper-year, student mentors from all 4 faculties, both at the undergraduate and the graduate level. We're happy to share our experiences and help you find the information you’re looking for leading up to and throughout your first year!

Meet the Mentors

Here's what we can help with:

Whether you have questions about using the course registration system, how to make friends at university or what resources are available to you, the mentors have you covered!

Chat with a mentor

Get answers to all your Concordia questions: meet with a mentor one-on-one via video call on Zoom.

 

Please note: Mentors are not authorized to provide immigration-related advice or tell you which courses to register for.

Book a Zoom call

Drop by our offices

Mentors are present at both campuses. Drop by for a quick chat to learn all about being a Concordia student before the start of term. 

 

SGW: Monday to Thursday, 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Fridays, 9 a.m. to 1 p.m.

Loyola: Monday to Thursday, 11 a.m. to 3 p.m.
