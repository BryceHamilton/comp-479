Hydro-Québec energy and expertise are revolutionizing the transportation industry

By developing innovative battery and motor technology for electric vehicles as well as a charging network, and producing clean and renewable energy, Hydro-Québec is actively involved in transportation electrification.

Owners of electric vehicles not only save money, but also contribute to reducing oil imports. For Hydro-Québec, promoting electric transportation supports decarbonization efforts in Québec.
