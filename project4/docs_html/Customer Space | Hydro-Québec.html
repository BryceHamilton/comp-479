Customer Space

The Customer Space is an essential tool to manage your accounts and understand your electricity consumption. Create yours today and you’ll have access to over 20 online services.

Take advantage of our online services

Log in to your Customer Space to pay your electricity bill without transaction fees. Don’t have a Customer Space yet? Now’s the time to create one!

    What will you need?

    • Contract holder: Your most recent bill.
    • Proxy holder or account manager: A recent bill of the account you manage.

Video: Your Customer Space

The Customer Space is an essential tool for managing your accounts and understanding your electricity consumption. It is a secure, easy-to-use way to access, in just a few clicks, more than 20 online services. For each of your accounts, you can view your account summary, your bill and payment history, your hourly electricity consumption and much more.
