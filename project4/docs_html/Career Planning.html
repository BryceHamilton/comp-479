    Level Up

    Level UP is a new program from Riipen that gives you access to complete short-term paid projects from real organizations putting your still to work.

    Get more information and sign-up today

    Humanities+

    Work-integrated Learning experience for students in Humanities-based programs at Concordia including Art History and Film Studies.

    Prepare for life after graduation, develop crucial life skills in 50-hour scheduled skills trainings and get 400 hours paid hands-on work experience in the summer.

    Get the program details

    Concordia Alumni Connect

    The Global Concordia Community is here for you!

    Being able to connect with others in your Concordia network is more important now than ever. By leaning on our community for support and advice, we can navigate these difficult times together. Alumni mentors can help you overcome this challenging time and ensure you don’t have to endure it alone. Whether you want to build your professional network, finally get some questions answered or map out your next career move, mentors can be a great resource.

    • Watch this short video to get the basics
    • Get started - set up your profile
    • Get noticed - make it easy for a mentor to find you
    Join Concordia Alumni Connect now!
