Ottawa Chapter Bursary Endowment

The Ottawa Chapter Bursary Endowment supports an annual entrance bursary of $500 for a Concordia undergraduate student from the Ottawa region. Your gift will continue to build our next bursary.

New York Chapter Award Endowment

Our goal is to create a scholarship at Concordia in the name of the New York chapter of the Concordia University Alumni Association, and in memory of Loyola alumnus Robert G. Beauregard, BA 60.

With this scholarship we seek to encourage more students to apply to Concordia.
